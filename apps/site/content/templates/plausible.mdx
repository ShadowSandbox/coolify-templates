## Overview

Plausible is an intuitive, lightweight, and open-source web analytics platform. No cookies, fully GDPR compliant, and the tracking script is under 1 KB — 75x smaller than Google Analytics.

This template deploys Plausible Community Edition with PostgreSQL for application data and ClickHouse for analytics storage.

## First steps

1. Open the generated domain and register the first admin account.
2. Add your website and copy the script tag into your site's `<head>`.
3. Optionally configure SMTP for email reports.
