## Overview

Uptime Kuma is a self-hosted monitoring tool like "Uptime Robot". Monitor HTTP(s), TCP, DNS, and Docker containers, build public status pages, and get notified through 90+ services including Telegram, Discord, and Slack.

This template deploys a single Uptime Kuma instance with a persistent volume for its data.
