## Overview

Sentry is the standard for self-hosted error tracking and performance monitoring. Capture exceptions, trace slow requests, and get release health across all your applications and services.

This template deploys the full self-hosted Sentry stack: the web dashboard, background workers, cron, Relay for event ingestion, plus Postgres, Redis, Kafka, and ClickHouse.
